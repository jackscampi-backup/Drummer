## 1. Stayin' Alive
- **Artista:** Bee Gees
- **Bassista:** Maurice Gibb
- **Album:** Saturday Night Fever (1977)
- **Tonalità:** Fm (4 bemolli)
- **Video:** 01:20

### Tablatura (3 battute)
```
G|--------------------------------|------1-------------------------|--------------------------------|
D|-----------------1--------------|--3-------3-------3---1---------|--3-----------------------------|
A|--------0---1---3---------------|--------------------------3-----|--------------------------------|
E|--------------------------------|--------------------------------|--------------------------------|
```

**Note:** Semplice ma iconico. Pickup notes (0-1-3) sulla corda A, poi D1. Battuta 2: D3, G1, D3, D3, D1, A3. Battuta 3: D3.

## 3. Le Freak

- **Album:** C'est Chic (1979)
- **Tonalità:** Am

```
    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
A|----------7-----------------------|----------------------------------|
E|------0-5---0---3-4---------------|--5-------------------------------|
```

### Ex.2 - Breakdown (3 battute)
```
|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|
D|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|-------------------------------|

E|--5---0-3-0-5-----5-3-5--------|--------0-3-5---------0-3------|--5-----------------------------|
```

### Ex.3 - Con trill/vibrato (4 battute)
```
    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
A|----X-5-5---7---7---5~(6)---------|--X-5-5---5---7---5~(6)-----------|
E|--5-----------------    ----5-----|--5-------------------    ----5---|

    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
A|----X-5-5---5---5~(6)-------------|--X-5-5---5---7-------------------|
E|--5-----------------    ----5-----|--5-----------    ---5-------(5)--|

X = nota muta, (6)/(5) = ghost note, tr/~ = trill
```

**Note:** Il riff più iconico del disco. Bernard Edwards doubled Nile Rodgers' guitar.

### Pattern principale (8 battute)

```
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|------3-5-------------------------|------3-5-------3-5---------------|

E|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|

    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|

```

### Slap Solo
```
    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
A|--3-X-3-X-X-X---X---3-3---3-5-----|--3-X-3-X-X-X-------3-5-X---3-5---|
E|----------------3-----------------|----------------------------------|
  T * T * T-|P T----| P             T * T * T-|P T----| T   P

    |1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|1 e + a 2 e + a 3 e + a 4 e + a|
G|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
D|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|
A|--3-X-3-X-X-X---X---3-3---3-5-----|--3-X-3-X-X-X---0-1-3-3/5---3-----|
E|----------------3-----------------|----------------------------------|
  T * T * T-|P T----| P             T * T * T-----------------|

* = Ghost note (drop fret-hand fingers across fretboard)
T = Thumb (slap)
P = Pop (pull)
```

**Note:** SLAP! Ottave e funk puro. Louis Johnson usava Music Man StingRay.
